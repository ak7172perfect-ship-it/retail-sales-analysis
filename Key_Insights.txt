Key Insights 
West & East regions contribute more than 60% of total sales
Technology is the best-performing category
Consumer segment generates the highest sales (~50%)
Sales peak in November–December (holiday season)
Profit is lowest in July

Product Insights
Phones and Storage are top-selling sub-categories
Canon product is the highest-selling item (~30K sales)

Business Recommendations
Focus on Technology products and Consumer segment
Improve performance in South & Central regions
Plan inventory for high sales during Nov–Dec
Work on improving profit margin (currently ~12%)
